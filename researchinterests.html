Origin of Sgr II (Upcoming, Summer 2018)

I will be using optical spectra taken with the IMACs spectrograph to determine the dark matter content of Sgr II, as well as its metallicity dispersion, to characterize it. I also hope to understand the origin and dynamics of this cluster using simulations.

Origin of 300S (Summer 2017-Present)

300S is a high-velocity stellar stream, whose kinematic signature was first discovered in narrow-field spectroscopic studies of Segue 1 (see Simon et al. 2011, Geha et al. 2010, Norris et al. 2009), and whose photometric counterpart was seen in matched-filter maps (see Niederste-Ostholt 2009, Bernard et al. 2016). I found members of 300S out to larger areas of the sky in wide-field spectroscopic surveys such as APOGEE-2, SEGUE-1 and SEGUE-2 using the stream's kinematic signature.
