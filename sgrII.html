The Origin of the Sgr II Satellite (Upcoming, Summer 2018)

Origin of Sgr II (Upcoming, Summer 2018)

Sgr II is a system discovered by Laevens et al. 2016 in PanSTARRS-1 data. With a half-light radius of 38 pc and luminosity of Mv = -5.2, Sgr II sits in the middle of the dwarf galaxy and globular cluster regimes on the size-luminosity plane.

I will be using optical spectra taken with the IMACs spectrograph to infer the dark matter content of Sgr II. I also hope to understand the origin and dynamics of this cluster using Gaia DR2 data and simulations.
